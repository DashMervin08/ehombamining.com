Our Product

Blue sodalite (Na8 (Al6Si6O24) Cl2) also know as Lapis Lazuli is our main supply.It is a gemstone that varies from deep blue to light green.It has various uses from industrial uses such as making beautiful tiles and countertops in construction, it is also used to make beautifull necklaces and gems. Blue sodalite also has healing abilities and can be used therapeutically.

QUALITY

Northern Namibia has some of the finest ores for Blue Sodalite thus we are very confident in the quality of our product. We take extra measures to ensure quality such as optimizing our extraction methods and transporting product straight to the warehouse for storage to avoid exposure to too much sunlight which may cause the product to lose its color if exposed for longer periods.

QUANTITY

Export quantity is primarily up to the buyer. We set no limits on the quantity of product the buyer wishes to purchase. We work hard to make sure that availability of product is always a certainty.

PROCEDURES

When a potential buyer shows clear and legit interest in purchasing our product we will send the buyer a sample for analysis. The sample will be accompanied by the following documents, Proforma invoice, Packing List and CO (Cerificate of origin).If the buyer is happy with the samples they can contact us to put in their order.We use LC (letter of Credit) so We will prepare the client's order upon recieving the LC from the bank. If the client prefers a different procedure we are open to negotiation.
